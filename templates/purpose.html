Purpose of the Research

This project investigates the relationship between CO₂ emissions and global temperature changes. By analyzing data on CO₂ levels and temperature variations, we aim to better understand how human activities impact the climate and contribute to global warming.

Through statistical analysis and visualizations, this research highlights the correlation between carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere and average temperature increases over time, emphasizing the need for sustainable practices and policy changes to mitigate climate change.
